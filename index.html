IHRD

IHRD is an autonomous educational institution established by the Government of Kerala in 1987. The institute is registered under the Travancore – Cochin Literary, Scientific and Charitable Societies Registration Act 12 of 1955. The Institute's Memorandum of Association and its rules and regulations have been judiciously framed with built-in growth provisions. The management of the institute is vested with a Governing Body composed of with the Hon’ble Minister of Higher Education, Government of Kerala, as the Chairman and Chief Secretary to Government of Kerala as the Vice-Chairman.

IHRD Fest

The IHRD National FEST is the Techno-Cultural- Entrepreneurial fest conducted by IHRD, aimed at providing a platform for improving the technical, non technical and cultural skills of the young engineering minds. The TECH FEST offers a plethora of events ranging from seminars and workshops on the state of art technology to contests that push student's talents and brains beyond the limits, thereby fetching the best brains from engineering institutions across the nation. It is the first time that IHRD is conducting a national Tech Fest.
